# samsung-global-sales-dashboard
Power BI Dashboard analyzing Samsung global sales (2021–2024)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes 22.9M total revenue generated across 52 countries between 2021–2024.  
The goal is to identify key growth drivers in terms of product categories, regions, and technology trends.

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies Used
- SQL – Data extraction, table joins, and data cleaning
- Excel – Initial data validation and exploration
- Power BI – Data modeling, DAX measures, and interactive dashboard development

---

## 📊 Dashboard Highlights

The dashboard provides insights into:

- Total Revenue Performance
- Top Revenue-Generating Product Category
- Revenue Distribution by Region
- 5G vs Non-5G Revenue Split
- Revenue vs Target Analysis
- Return Rate Analysis by Region

---

## 🔍 Key Business Insights

- Europe contributes over 30% of total revenue, making it the leading market.
- Smart TVs are the highest revenue-generating category.
- 5G products contribute approximately 32% of total revenue.
- The return rate is around 7–9%, indicating scope for operational improvement.

---

## 🚀 Business Recommendations

- Reduce return rates by identifying region-specific operational issues.
- Launch 5G upgrade campaigns to convert non-5G users.
- Increase focus on high-performing regions and product categories.
- Strengthen direct-to-consumer sales channels for better margins.
